About Me

Hello, my name is Léna, welcome to my site!

I am an interior designer and I love to draw. I created this site to share my passion for architectural drawing. I draw by hand on the iPad for fun and also to give life to the projects of other building professionals.

My ambience drawings help end customers to project themselves and therefore to win projects more easily. My clients are architects, decorators, building companies, investors and real estate agencies, space designers, landscapers etc…

Drawing also helps to imagine, to test ideas, to compose. So, if you also like drawings follow my site and join my universe

Do not hesitate to ask me questions if you are also interested in the architectural drawing.

Thank you for being here ! 🥰

Services

For a good visualization of interior design you need:

  • 01. The plan of the room
  • 02. Photos of the furniture chosen, the color of the walls and the floor covering
  • 03. The chosen decorative objects
  • 04. Photos of the existing if possible

After about 2 days, the visualization is ready. There is more to it than just making changes if necessary.
